Privacy Policy for AIM TO LEARN

This privacy policy explains how AIM TO LEARN handles information shared by visitors, students, and parents while using the website.

Last updated

July 11, 2026

Privacy policy overview

AIM TO LEARN is committed to keeping visitor information safe, clear, and appropriate for an educational website that may use ads in the future.

Information We Collect

We may collect basic information when visitors contact us or ask for help. This can include a name and email address when someone chooses to share it. We may also collect general information about how the site is used so we can improve our essays and learning materials.

Cookies

We use cookies to help the website work properly and to remember simple preferences. Cookies also help us understand how visitors use the site so we can keep it easy to navigate. You can manage cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Analytics

We may use simple analytics tools to see which pages are helpful and how visitors move around the site. This helps us improve the experience without identifying individual visitors.

Google AdSense Readiness

If we show ads in the future, we will follow privacy rules and keep our practices clear and fair. We aim to collect only the information that is needed and to make sure the site remains suitable for educational use.

Third-party Links

AIM TO LEARN may include links to other websites for extra learning. These websites are run by other organisations and may have their own privacy practices. Please check their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

Downloadable Files

PDF files are hosted directly from the /downloads folder. These files are static and do not collect personal data. When downloading, please use a secure connection and scan files with your own security tools if needed.

Children's Privacy

AIM TO LEARN is intended for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If a parent or guardian believes a child has shared information without permission, please contact us right away.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse or unauthorised access. Because the site is mostly static, we keep personal information to a minimum and only keep it when it is needed for support or communication.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact AIM TO LEARN at support@aimtolearn@gmail.com. We are happy to answer privacy questions and update our practices when needed.

Policy Updates

AIM TO LEARN may update this privacy policy when our website or legal requirements change. We will post the latest date on this page, and continued use of the website means you accept any updates.
